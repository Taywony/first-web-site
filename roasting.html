Roasting

  1. Hot Air Roaster: A machine that uses hot air to roast the beans.
  2. Drum Roaster: A machine that rotates the beans inside a drum, using direct heat to roast them.
  3. Popcorn Popper: A popular DIY method that uses a hot air popcorn popper to roast small batches of coffee.
  4. Oven Roasting: Roasting the beans in an oven, although this method is not recommended as it's difficult to achieve a consistent roast.

Regardless of the method, the general process of roasting coffee involves heating the beans to a certain temperature for a specific amount of time to achieve a desired roast level (light, medium, dark, etc.).
